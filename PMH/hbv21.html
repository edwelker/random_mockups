Abatacept (Injection)

a-BAT-a-sept

Summary:

Treats moderate to severe rheumatoid arthritis for adults who have taken other medicines for RA. Also treats moderate to severe juvenile idiopathic arthritis in children 6 years of age and older.

What works?

This summary of a Cochrane review presents what we know from research about the effects of abatacept on rheumatoid arthritis... Read more

UsesSide effects

Abatacept injection is used alone or together with other medicines to reduce the signs and symptoms of moderate to severe rheumatoid arthritis. Abatacept helps keep joint damage from getting worse after other medicines have been used and did not work well... Read more
